## Testing Checklist

When testing, verify:

- [ ] Greetings are detected and responded to appropriately
- [ ] Club questions provide the Club Directory link
- [ ] Wake Tech eligibility questions provide the eligibility page link
- [ ] Wake Tech dual credit questions provide the dual credit chart link
- [ ] Wake Tech FAQ questions provide the FAQs page link
- [ ] General Wake Tech questions may or may not have links (depending on specificity)
- [ ] Course questions provide accurate course information
- [ ] Graduation requirement questions provide correct credit requirements (22 required, 26 recommended)
- [ ] Out-of-scope questions are politely declined
- [ ] Links are properly formatted as markdown links
- [ ] Answers are focused and don't include unnecessary information
- [ ] Answers use markdown formatting (bold for important numbers, headers, etc.)
